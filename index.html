Volcanoes World Wide

Welcome to our data visualization of volcanic eruptions! Our interactive tool allows users to explore the frequency and distribution of volcanic eruptions around the world, as well as the characteristics of individual volcanoes.

We hope that this data visualization will provide users with a deeper understanding of the patterns and trends in volcanic activity, and will serve as a valuable resource for those interested in the science of volcanoes. Thank you for using our tool, and we hope you find it informative and enjoyable.
